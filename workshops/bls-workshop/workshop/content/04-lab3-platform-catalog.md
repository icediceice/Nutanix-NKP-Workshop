## Goal

Explore NKP's **Platform Application Catalog** — the curated set of platform services
(observability tools, ingress controllers, databases, security) that any team can deploy
across clusters from a single control plane.

> **This is a facilitator-guided session.** Open the tools below and follow along.

---

## Step 1 — Open Kommander

1. Click **<a href="https://kommander.nkp.nuth-lab.xyz" target="_blank">Open Kommander ↗</a>**.
2. Navigate to **Clusters** → click `workload01`.
3. In the cluster sidebar, click **Applications**.

Your facilitator will walk you through what you see here.

---

## Step 2 — Open the Lab Manager

Your facilitator will direct you to the lab environment dashboard.

Keep both tabs open — you will switch between them during the walkthrough.
