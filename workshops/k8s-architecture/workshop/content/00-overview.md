## What We're Doing

This workshop is the core Kubernetes deep-dive. You will work through sixteen topics covering
every major Kubernetes object type. By the end you will be able to deploy, configure, secure,
observe, and scale production-grade applications on a real cluster.

## Prerequisites

- Completion of the K8s Introduction workshop
- A running namespace assigned to your session (pre-configured)

## Conventions Used in This Workshop

Blocks labelled **Try It Yourself** are optional extensions. The facilitator will indicate
whether time permits. All terminal blocks are executable — click them or press Enter in the
embedded terminal.
